Thriving as an Event Volunteer at the PEO Technical Symposium 2024!
  • by:Sujan Silwal
  • date:June 24, 2024

Volunteering at the PEO Technical Symposium 2024 was an exhilarating experience, packed with opportunities to learn, network, and grow. As a PEO student volunteer, I had the chance to collaborate with industry experts and fellow volunteers, creating an unforgettable morning of technical talks and professional development.

Unveiling the Symposium Highlights

1.Cloud-Based CAD: An Engineering Tool You Can't Afford to Miss

  • Speaker: Ian Gardiner, PEng, Senior Systems Engineer at ATS Automation.
  • Overview: IIan Gardiner took us on a journey through the evolution of CAD technology, spotlighting the transition to cloud-based CAD. His insights on how modern CAD tools revolutionize design accuracy and collaboration were truly eye-opening.

    • 2.Simulation Modeling: Predicting Outcomes in a Chaotic System

  • Speaker: Gerda Trollip, PEng, Senior Simulation Engineer at MOSIMTEC LLC.
  • Overview: Gerda Trollip captivated the audience with her deep dive into simulation modeling. She demonstrated its critical role in predicting outcomes and informed decision-making, backed by compelling case studies from various industries.

    • 3.Empowering Ontario's Energy Future: Innovations, Challenges, and Opportunities

  • Speaker: Kareem Nakhla, Business Developer at Attain Energy .
  • Overview: Kareem Nakhla’s talk on Ontario’s energy future was both inspiring and informative. He covered renewable energy adoption, grid modernization, and the transformative power of digitalization, highlighting the path towards a sustainable energy future.
